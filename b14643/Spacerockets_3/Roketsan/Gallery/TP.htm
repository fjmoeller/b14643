Teknoloji Platformu 0.2 (TP 0.2)



TP 0.2-1 launch

  Under the leadership of Roketsan, a group of technicians have developed a two-stage sounding rocket,
 called Teknoloji Platformu.
 So far, three of them have been launched.  TP 0.2-1 and TP 02-2 visually differ from TP 02-3 by painting.
 The first stage of the TP is a solid fuel motor of 0.6 m diameter (Yildirim missile ?).
 It looks like the second stage uses liquid propellant.
 The TP 0.2-3 reached an altitude of more than 130 km.


Source of pictures: Volkan Miguyan and Roketsan
