Rocket Factory Augsburg GmbH (RFA) * OHB Group

 


"Rocket Factory Augsburg GmbH (RFA) was founded on the belief that opening fair and affordable access to space will remove boundaries limiting human evolution. RFA is developing a launch service for small satellites to space in a unique set up. Rocket Factory Augsburg GmbH (RFA) is part of the OHB Group with headquarter in Augsburg. With our experienced core team, we are focusing on affordable launch technology within an automotive manufacturing environment. The company is operating for one year with already 50 employees and promising growth perspectives."

It is confirmed that the two-stage launch vehicle has a diameter of 2 m and is 30 m long. A total of 9 identical engines are used. Each engine is based on a simple gas generator cycle. The turbo pump is said to have already been tested. Fuel and thrust are still unknown. A third kick-stage propulsion is based on green propellants with pressure propulsion.
The launch vehicle is expected to launch from a platform in the North Sea (proposed by OHB). The payload should be 1200 kg for a 700 km SSO.

New informations:

The engine development is switched from a open gas generator cycle to a staged combustion.

RFA purchased the power head (turbopump+preburner) of RD-8 engine manufactured by Yuzhmash.
Source: Unnamed insider from Ukraine

Now the staged combustion RFA engine is based on RD-8 components. RFA firing tests were conducted using this hardware.
