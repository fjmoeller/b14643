2020

The new Firefly Beta conception is an evolutionary
design based on Firefly Alpha technology.
Beta is a two-stage launch vehicle capable of delivering
8,000 kg to a 200 km LEO and has the capability of
achieving a GTO.
The Beta vehicle utilizes technologies such as all carbon
composite tanks.
