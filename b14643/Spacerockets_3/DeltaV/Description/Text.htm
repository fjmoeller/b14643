DeltaV NLV
 
 

  DeltaV Uzay Teknolojileri A.S.

 

The private company DeltaV Uzay Teknolojileri A.S. plans to develop a small launcher for Earth orbit. The basis is a recently presented hybrid motor made of carbon material with a diameter of 0.62 m. It will first be tested in flight in the Sounding rocket SORS (Sonda roket sistemi).
The launcher has three stages. In the first Stage 5 pods of the SORS are to be bundled.
