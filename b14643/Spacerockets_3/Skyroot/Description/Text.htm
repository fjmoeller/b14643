Skyroot Aerospace

 

Skyroot Aerospace is a Hyderabad-based company. Three launch vehicles – Vikram I, II, and III – can carry payloads ranging from 300 kg to 700 kg to low Earth orbit.

All Vikram variants have solid motors for the lower three stages (Kalam-1000, Kalam-250, Kalam-100). Their initial Vikram-I would have the uppermost (fourth) stage powered by N2O4/MMH and  "Raman" engines. A future Vikram-III would feature solid strap-ons as well.
Currently, the team is going to test a 3D printed upgraded Raman engine (Raman-II) and a fully composite (carbon fiber) and high-performance solid rocket motor Kalam-100.
