Taiwan Innovative Space Inc. * ATSpace/Southern Launch


2022 -- TiSpace has relocated its activities to Australia and is working there in cooperation with ATSpace/Southern Lauch to continue its program.


TiSace (Taiwan Innovative Space) is the first private company in Taiwan. TiSPACE is established to provide innovative and cost-effective launch services to the global space community with increasing commercial needs of placing Nano- and Micro-satellites into the low Earth orbits (LEO).
The headquarter of TiSpace is located in Chunan, Miaoli that is in close vicinity with Taiwan high-tech science parks and industrial districts. TiSpace was established in 2016 by a group of rocket scientists, aerospace engineers and entrepreneurs who saw the emerging great opportunities in space privatization and technical and economic future. Among them, Dr. Yen-Sen Chen, is a key technical person who is a well-known researcher in the international space launch community. He has an extensive experience in the technical developments of hybrid rocket propulsion and launch vehicle systems. Dr. Chen initiated the hybrid rocket system developments of Taiwan in 2009.

Since no approval was given for launch of the Hapith launchers in Taiwan, TiSpace and all of its equipment moved to Whaler's Way (Australia). The first launch is announced for September 2021.
 

2021, Sept. 16 -- Failure. The launcher suffered an internal fault causing the vehicle to catch alight.
„After inspection of the Hapith-I vehicle, post the September 16th launch attempt, Southern Launch and TiSpace have concluded that we won’t continue with any further launch attempts of this particular vehicle.“
