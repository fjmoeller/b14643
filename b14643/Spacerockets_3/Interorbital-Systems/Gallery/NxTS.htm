IOS Neptune NxTS (conception 2020)


The configuration of the elements for the NEPTUNE rocket was completely changed again. The version CPM-2.0 will not be further developed.
Now an each standard Neptune Building Block (NBB) is composed of four propellant tanks (two fuel and two oxidizer tanks). Each two-stage future NEPTUNE rocket variant consists of two or more NBBs arranged side-by-side in parallel or stacked in tandem.
IOS has eliminated the requirement for a pump by developing ultra-lightweight filament-wound propellant tanks and by utilizing high-density propellants that substantially reduce the size of the propellant tanks.
The first orbital version (N2TS) consists of two NBBs with one engine each. A small upper stage with cold-gas ACS will startet inside the hole between the first stage tanks.
