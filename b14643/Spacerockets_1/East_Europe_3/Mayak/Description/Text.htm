Mayak
 
 

National Space Agency of Ukraine * KB Yushnoye
 
NPO Yushnoye is to develop of Mayak satellite launch vehicles based on technologies used on the Tsiklon and the Zenit 2 booster. The Mayak  launcher family will be powered by two or four  NPO Energomash RD-120M engines, a second  stage using one RD-120 and a third stage powered by four Yushnoye RD-809M engines ( based on RD-8 vernier engines). Mayak-12  will be able to place 1.7 tons into 500 km SSO with a first flight planned in about 2005. A longer Mayak-23 would be able to loft 3.0 tons into GTO.
Yushnoye says it is considering launch sites at Alcantara and at Overberg Test Range (South Africa).
