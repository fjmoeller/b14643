German experimental engine for the A-4 (B-variant)
The Germans had developed the large centrally-mounted injector plate intended
for use in a new head combustion chamber engine for the A-4. The injector
featured a circular slotted plate with orifice holes arranged in complex radial,
parallel and circular patterns.
This never went into production, but formed the basis for successful post-war
American rocket A-4 engine.
