Some identified rocket-engines
from Isayev's Design Bureau (now KB Khimmash)

Norbert Brügge, Germany

Update: 05.09.2011

Throughout its history the Isayev Design Bureau OKB-2 has created since 1953 more than 120 liquid-fuel engines and engine assemblies for missiles and spacecrafts.
Approximately 40 of these engine designs proceeded into serial production. Here are some data and pictures, which I found up to now.
Some of them are similar to Isayev's engines, but of other designers (e.g. OKB Sevruk; in 1958 with Isayev's OKB-2 fusioned)*.
