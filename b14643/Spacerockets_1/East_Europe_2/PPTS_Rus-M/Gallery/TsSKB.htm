TsSKB Progress


          


Proposal by TsSKB Progress for a new space transport system Rus-M
with the application a Universal Rockets Module (URB) and RD-0163, RD-171 and RD-0120 engines


  Rus-M L:    Shortened URB with an engine RD-0163 for a payload of 3 t (LEO). As second stage the Block - I of the Soyuz-2 is used.
  Rus-M S1:   URB with two engines RD-0163 for a payload of 6 t (LEO). As second stage the Block - I of the Soyuz-2 is used.
  Rus-M S2:   URB with two engines RD-0163 for a payload of 8 t (LEO). The new second stage uses four engines RD-0146.
  Rus-M P1:   Central URB with an engine RD-0163 and two additional URB's as booster with respectively two engines RD-0163. Capability 20 t for LEO.
  Rus-M P2:   Central URB with cryogenic fuel and an engine RD-0120 as well as two additional URB's as booster with respectively two engines RD-0163
                     or an engine RD-171. Capability 22 t for LEO.

  Rus-M T:    Central URB with cryogenic fuel and an engine RD-0120 as well as four additional URB's as booster with respectively two engines RD-0163
                     or an engine RD-171. Capability 40 t for LEO.
  Rus-M ST:   Central URB of clearly larger dimension. Cryogenic fuel and four engines RD-0120 as well as six additional URB's as booster
                     with respectively two engines RD-0163 or one engine RD-171. Capability 100 t for LEO.
