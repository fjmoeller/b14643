Note: The Block DM-2M-03 (=Dm3, 11S861-03) upper stage was developed primarily to increase the performance of Zenit-3SL rockets.

With 2,000 kilograms of extra propellant, which was believed to be mistakenly loaded onboard, the tanks would be filled close to the same level as the old ones - the oxygen tank would be 90 percent full with a 25-percent reserve load.
