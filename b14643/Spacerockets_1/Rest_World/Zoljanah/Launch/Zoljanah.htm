2022, June, 14 -- Satellite images from MAXAR shows preparations for another launch of the Zoljanah SLV.
 In the morning is the first stage already erected on the launch pad. The transporter with the upper part

 of the rocket is waiting. In the afternoon the stack is complete. Two cranes were used for the stacking.


       

2022, February -- We note that Iran attempted another launch that failed.

 There is a big burn mark at the site. Possibly another Zoljanah SLV.
