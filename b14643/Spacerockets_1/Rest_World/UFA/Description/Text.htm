2018, January -- In Turkey, two projects will be realized in parallel.
UFA space rocket project.
UFA is a project of SSM (Secretariat for Defense Industry) and the Air Force and is not civil. The rocket will secretly developed and should be ready to launch in 2023. It should then be able to bring 1.5t in a 700km SSO.

BURAK sounding (space) rocket project.
The client is UDH (Ministry of Transport). Roketsan develops the rocket and associated launch systems. The project will start in 2018. From 2020, research satellites (10 kg) will be put into orbit.

2017, Dec. 04 -- The Turkish Ministry of Transport, Maritime Affairs and Communications is reportedly preparing to launch a national satellite launch vehicle (SLV) program in 2018. According to the Daily Sabah and Anadolu Agency, the Space Probe Rocket and Launch System (BURAK) program aims to end Turkey’s reliance on foreign SLV providers. Specific parameters of the BURAK SLV program, such as payload capacity, are not known.

2017, May -- Roketsan has now presented his plan for the development of an orbital rocket. The UFA (Uydu Firlatma Araci) vehicle is two-stage and uses completely liquid fuel.

 


Satellite Ejection System (UFS) project
Authority: Turkish Air Force.
Scope of the project is the establishment of a satellite launch center and development of an UFS.

Satellite launching system (UFS) project preliminary conceptual design (phase 1) agreement was signed between Roketsan and Undersecretariat of Defence Industries. In accordance with the signature the process to carry Turkish satellite to space has officially started. The phase will be completed whithin a year.The system will carry 1500 kg payload to 700 km altitude as orbit.
