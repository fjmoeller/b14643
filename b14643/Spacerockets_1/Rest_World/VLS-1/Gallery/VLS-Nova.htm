VS-15 SAMF

 

Specification
The Liquid Propulsion Rocket Engine converts the chemical energy contained in the tanks filled with propellant liquid, ethyl alcohol (ethanol) and LOX (Liquid Oxygen), into a propulsive force to generate a force up to 15,000 N.

Dimensions and Type:
Nozzle Type: “Contoured Nozzle” bell type
Nozzle Output Diameter: 265 mm
Nozzle Length: 170 mm
Fuel pressure: 28.5 atm
Oxidizer pressure: 23.5 atm
Fuel mass flow rate: 2.44 kg/s
Oxidizer mass flow rate: 3.82 kg/s
Fuel temperature: 30°C
Oxidizer temperature: -183°C
Chamber Pressure: 16.5 atm
Chamber Maximum Pressure: 18.5 atm
