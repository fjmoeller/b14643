TLV

The TLV is a rocket for test the in-flight performance of the Hanwha Techwin's 75-ton and 7-ton thrust rocket engines. The maiden flight will be in 2018, November (28).
Technical specifications of the TLV:  26.1 or 25.8m length, 2.6m diameter, 53 tons total mass, 38 tons propellant. (see "Versions").
