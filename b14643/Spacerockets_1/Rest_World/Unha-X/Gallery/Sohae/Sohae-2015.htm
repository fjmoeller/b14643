Upgrade of the Sohae Space Launch Ground in 2014/2015

After the last launch of the Unha-3 in 2012 the Sohae Space Launch Facility has been significantly changed. First, the launch tower was extended about 14 m. It followed the construction of a railway line, which is introduced in a tunnel, that have at the end upwards a slot. This means that no longer carried out the transportation of rocket stages to the launch site on the road. The rocket parts are lifted by a rail transfer platform out the slot in the tunnel by an internal crane, and transported directly to the launch tower or temporarily stored in a new support building. The transfer platform has a hinged roof, so that the rocket parts can be erected by the crane of the launch tower.
