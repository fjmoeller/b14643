Project IRIS
 
 

 

IRIS = Islamic Republic Iran Satellite

 

As early as 1988 Iran announced its objective to establish a space program. Iranian source reports that when Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei visited a defense fair the same year, images of a SLV surfaced that apparently showed a SLV resembling the Shahab-3 with the letters "IRIS" painted on it.
Iranian sources characterized the IRIS launcher as being "liquid and solid fueled" but it is known that the Shahab-3 missile consisted of a single liquid propellant stage. A smaller solid propellant second stage were added (see below). A launch vehicle of this configuration is ideal as a vertical probe sounding rocket but would almost certainly not be capable of launching a satellite of appreciable mass.

Therefore, the project was ultimately not implemented.
