Ghaem-100
 
 

Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps  (IRGC)

 

 

2022, November 05 -- Iran's IRGC has announced that they has made successfully a test-flight of the rocket designated as "Ghaem-100" from the Shahrud launch site. The information that the first stage is a recently tested new "Raafe" solid-fuel motor allow for a good analysis based on the published photos.

The rocket is only about 15.5 m high overall. The "Raafe" stage has a diameter of 1.3 m. This can be determined from the second stage ("Salman"), which has a diameter of 1.0 m. Except for the first stage, the Ghaem-100 corresponds to the "Qased" from IRGC.

However, it is hard to imagine that the presented "Ghaem-100" will be able to carry the advertised 80 kg payload into a 500 km orbit. Therefore, an orbital version of the "Ghaem-100" will be required, somewhat by doubling the first stage.
