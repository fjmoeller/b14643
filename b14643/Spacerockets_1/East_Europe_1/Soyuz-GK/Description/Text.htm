Soyuz  GK Launch Services
 
 

GK Launch Services * TsSKB Progress * GLAVKOSMOS

 

GK Launch Services is a company established by Glavkosmos (a subsidiary of ROSCOSMOS State Space Corporation) and International Space Company Kosmotras. GK Launch Services is an new operator of Soyuz-2 commercial launches from the Russian spaceports.

 

The first launch of a Soyuz-2.1a / Fregat under the direction of GK LS will take place in March 2021. The rocket gets a new design in white-blue (instead of gray-orange).
