Soyuz-2 (2.1a)  & Soyuz-2K (2.1b)
 
 

 

In further development the Soyuz-U and Soyuz-FG are modified to the variant Soyuz-2. The Soyuz-2 will replace the given up project Rus (Soyuz - M) in his original form. The already modified engines RD-107A/RD-108A are taken over by the Soyuz-FG. The third stage block I is improved gradually. First all the tanks to changed ratio of the propellant components become adjusted (Soyuz-2 or  Soyuz-2.1a). In the second phase the engine RD-0110 is replaced by a RD-0124  (Soyuz-2K or Soyuz-2.1b). As additional fourth stage can be used the "Fregat".

The first launch of the Soyuz-2 (1a) resulted at that 08.11.2004. It was a qualification - test of the rocket without payload. For the launch a Yantar-Fairing was used. The first payload (MetOp) was started by STARSEM at that 19.10.2006 successfully. As payload fairing the new model of STARSEM was used with a diameter of 4.1 m and a length of 11.4 m.
The Russian military will accomplish the next launch of the Soyuz-2 (1a) in December 2006 from Plesetzk. As payload fairing for the new communications satellite "Meridian -1" will use probably an enlarged STARSEM SF-fairing. It has a diameter of 3.72 m and a length of 10.20 (10.37 ?) m. Meridian - 1 should replace the Molnya communication - satellites.
