CZ-9 (RLV)


This future reusable launch vehicle, named CZ-9, has completely new engines and no lateral boosters are added.
In the meantime, however, two versions are already known.

The first version uses for the first stage 8x the double-chamber YF-135 engine (with 660 t thrust each) that work with methane / LOX.The YF-120t engines for two upper stages that work with LH2 / LOX are also new.

The second version have completely cryogenic engines (H2/LOX), which are still under development and whose designation is still missing.The thrust of the single-chamber engine should be 120 t. The first stage has 26 engines,
the second has 4, and the third has 1.
