Chang Zheng      CZ-X HLV
 
 

China Academy of Launch Vehicle Technology (CALT)

 

    

 

In addition to the super-heavy rocket SHLV (CZ-9), China is planning another HLV (CZ-X) launcher for crew transport. Probably this rocket will be used together with the CZ-9 especially for the lunar landing program.

The core and two boosters are each equipped with four engines (YF-100?). Corresponding to the diameter of 5 m, the launcher corresponds to a stretched CZ-5.
The new Crew Transport System weighs 14 tons and, together with the service module, is 7.23 m long (see CZ-7). For the lunar mission, the service module is modified for more power.
