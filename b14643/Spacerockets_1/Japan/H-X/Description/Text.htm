JAXA has begun to study the next generation launch vehicle, which aims for significantly reduced cost and higher reliability comparable to the human transportation mission. The newest project of the H-X has two kinds of 4m and 5.2m diameter.
4m diameter H-X composes of one LE-X engine for the 1st stage and one LE-5B or MB60 engine for the 2nd stage. The 4m diameter H-X also will be equipped with a small upper stage
LNG (LE-8). The fuel weight of the H-X 1st stage in 4m diameter is 120 ton. If  the 4m H-X vehicle uses two "1st stage" as its booster stages and matches 2nd stage of LE-5B or MB60 , it has 23 ton LEO capacility. As soon as H-X will be launched in 2018.
