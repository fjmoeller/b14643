2016, April 24 -- North Korea said a submarine-launched solid-fuel ballistic missile test it conducted had been a "great success". North Korea fired the missile from a submarine off its east coast on April 23 and it flew for about 30 km, a South Korean Defence Ministry official said. The North Korean State TV also showed what it claimed were underwater images of the missile being ejected from the underwater tube.
