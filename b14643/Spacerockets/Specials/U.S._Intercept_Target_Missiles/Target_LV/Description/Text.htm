MDA Target Launch Vehicle
 
 

Missile Defense Agency

The Ground-Based Midcourse Defense (GMD) system, originally known as the National Missile Defense (NMD) program, is one segment of the United States' forthcoming Ballistic Missile Defense System. As such it is managed by the Missile Defense Agency (MDA), formerly known as Ballistic Missile Defense Organization (BMDO).

TLV= Target Launch Vehicle

Minotaur-II TLV
The Minotaur-II Target launch vehicle (TLV) being produced by Orbital Sciences Corp. for the USAF and USArmy under the Orbital/Suborbital Program (OSP) provides a low-cost, reliable target solution for Ground Based Midcourse (GBM) testing. The vehicle utilizes residual Minuteman-II boosters coupled with avionics similar to the flight-proven Minotaur-I and Pegasus space launch vehicles. Mintour-II has a length of 19.55 m. The shroud is 4.26 m long.
New TLV variants: Minotaur-II+ (=Minuteman-III), Minotaur-Lite, Minotaur-II Heavy

Polaris A3 (STARS= Strategic Target System)
A long-range Strategic Targets System (STARS) rocket is launched from the Kodiak Launch Complex on Kodiak Island, Alaska on February 24, 2006. The rocket was successfully tracked by the Beale AFB radar during the exercise, and target data, including a weapon task plan (firing solution), was successfully transmitted  into the Command, Control, Battle Management and Communication system for the Ballistic Missile Defense System.

Three stage target vehicle consisting of a surplus Polaris A3 SLBM (A3P first stage + Hercules X-260 second stage) and an Orbus-1 third stage.  Sandia National Laboratories began development of the vehcile in 1985. The STARS I configuration could boost as single simulated warhead.

STARS II added the ODES (Operations and Deployment Experiments Simulator) fourth stage, which simulated the post-boost vehicle of an ICBM and could dispense decoys or multiple simulated warheads.
Manufacturer: Lockheed. Total Mass: 16 700 kg (36 800 lb). Core Diameter: 1.37 m (4.49 ft). Total Length: 11.50 m (37.70 ft).

Poseidon C3 TLV

Launch vehicle is a modified Poseidon-C3 SLBM used mainly for simulating missile warheads for MDA.

 

Trident C4 TLV
