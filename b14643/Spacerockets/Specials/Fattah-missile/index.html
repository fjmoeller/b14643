Iran's newest missiles „Khyber Shekan“ and „Fattah“ in the Fateh-110 category

Norbert Brügge, Germany


The analysis of photos of this two missiles shows a basic agreement in the dimensions. Both core motors have a diameter of 0.81m and are 8.8m long. The key difference is the RV. Fattah's RV is stretched 1.0m to improve aerodynamics. Also the kick motor (probably Arash-24) with a swiveling nozzle is an important aspect for the maneuverability of the RV. The range of both missiles is significantly greater than previous ones in this missile-category.
