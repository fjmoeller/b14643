North Korea's Ghost-Missiles in empty canisters on parades

Norbert Brügge, Germany
 

The vehicles with huge canisters for solid-fuel missiles shown at parades in 2017 and now 2023 can be classified as propaganda. North-Korea has not yet developed or tested such long motors with this diameter. Corresponding missiles are also not known. Consequently, the canisters carried are empty and the supposed missiles belong in the realm of fantasy.
Note: The recently tested motor PS180 is not a long motor and is only suitable for the PS-5 or PS-6.
