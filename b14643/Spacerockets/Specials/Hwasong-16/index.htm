North Korea's advanced long range missile "Hwasong-16" (HS-16)

Norbert Brügge, Germany
 

2022, October 10 -- Now there are photos of the October 4th launch of a missile likely related to the HS-12. It is single stage (see cable duct) like this one and was launched from the same TEL. However, the missile to be designated here as HS-16 has a different WH. The key difference, however, is the apparent missing of control thrusters. This can only mean that the single "Pektusan" engine is swiveling mounted.
