The new solid-fuel missile Pukguksong-6 GLBM

An extended variant of the PS-4 and PS-5

Norbert Brügge, Germany

2022, April 25 -- North Korea presented a silo-based Pukguksong-6 GLBM at the parade. It is based on the predecessors PS-4 and PS-5 and probably has a 2 m longer motor as the 1st stage.
