Landspace ZQ-2
 
 


Landspace announced their new concepted methalox medium-lift launch vehicle Zhuque-2 and its powerplant, the in-house manufactured methalox engine Tianque-2 in the first China Astronautical Congress. ZQ-2 will be reusable, the know-how is confidential now. They will demonstrated reusable technology in maiden launch, wait until 2020 or 2021.
