DW-2
 
 
Rocket Pi * JiuZhouYunJian
 

The "Rocket Pi" company is completely unknown in rocket activities. It surprises in August 2022 with already tested engines for a planned two-stage launch vehicle, called DW-2. The launcher should have a length of 27.2 m. With a diameter of 2.25 m, the rocket has a take-off weight of 53 t.

A single GG-engine "Longyun-70" with a thrust of 676 kN and 291 sec Isp is planned for the first stage. The combination methane/LOX is used as fuel.
The methalox engine "Lingyun-10V" (thrust 122 kN, Isp 345 sec) is intended for the second stage.
Both engines were developed and tested by JiuZhouYunJian (JZYJ) in Bengbu.

The DW-2 is designed to place 470 kg payload in a 300 km LEO.
