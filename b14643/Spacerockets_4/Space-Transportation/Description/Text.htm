Jiageng-1
 
 

Space Transportation Technology Co. Ltd.
 

Xiamen University has test flown on April 23rd, 2019 a hypersonic waverider mockup dubbed XTER-1 atop a reusable winged rocket launcher called Jiageng-1. The launcher has been co-developed by Space Transportation Technology. The 8.5 m long Jiageng-1 to reach a max altitude of 26.2 km and max speed of 4,300 km/h.The propulsion system is a solid-fuel motor.

Another test took place in December 2019.

 

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

This tests are steps toward the development of the two-stage Tianxing-1. The TX-1 is testing a hypersonic engine for use in a reusable spaceplane. The first test flight took place on January 24, 2022. After testing the hypersonic engine (the second stage was not separated), the landing took place with a parachute.

 

Note: According to a report, a second mission on August 26 placed a payload "Space Transportation Technology" on a suborbital trajectory that subsequently was landed at an airfield. It is very likely that a Tianxing-1 was launched.
