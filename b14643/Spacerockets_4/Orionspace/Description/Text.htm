Orionspace
 

Orionspace is a company that intends to develop a monstrous launch vehicle of 31m in height, based on the bundling of solid-fuel motors except for the third kick stage. The core stage and 4 attached boosters are of the same caliber with a diameter of 2.65 m. The statements of 150 and 255 tons of thrust are contradictory. The oversized fairing has a dimension of 11 x 4 m.
The take-off weight is given as 400 t. The capacity should be 4.2 t for a SSO (500 km).
