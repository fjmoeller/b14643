Explore-1
 
 
Beijing Space Trek Technology Co. Ltd. ------------------> Beijing Starway Technology Co. Ltd.

 

 The single-stage solid rocket "Explore-1" is the first product from Beijing Starway Exploration Technology Co., Ltd.
and is to be used for tests of the "China Science and Technology City Star" hypersonic vehicle.
In parallel, a multi-stage solid-stage rocket "Starway-1" with orbital capability is being developed. The next step is to develop liquid rocket engines.

Explore-1 has a diameter of 1.0 m, a height of 7.6 m and a weight of 5.8 t (6.5 t with payload). The liftoff thrust is given as 159 kN.
This information is suitable for an solid motor L-SpaB-100.
