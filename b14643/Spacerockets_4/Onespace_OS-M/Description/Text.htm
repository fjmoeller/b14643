Onespace OS-M
 
 

2019, March -- OneSpace has completed the assembly on the first OS-M orbital rocket scheduled to launch from Jiuquan during the final week of March. But the launch on March 27, 2019 was not successful because the launcher went out of control after separation from the first stage after 45.7 seconds.
